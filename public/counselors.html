FAQ

Common questions

How is this different from other major quizzes?

Most major quizzes use simple category matching — they ask a few questions and return the same 5 answers to every creative student or every analytical student. FindYourMajor uses Claude AI (Anthropic) to analyze the full combination of a student's interests, work style, values, strengths, and dealbreakers simultaneously. The result is genuinely personalized — including a wildcard pick the student almost never expected.

What data is stored about my students?

Quiz answers are stored temporarily (maximum 24 hours) to generate the Parent Report if one is purchased, then permanently deleted. We do not store student names, emails, or any personally identifiable information. Students can take the quiz anonymously. This design makes the platform FERPA-friendly by default.

How does the Parent Report work?

When a student completes the quiz through your counselor link, they can share their results with a parent. The parent can then purchase a full 10-section Parent Report for $14.99 — covering major deep-dives, salary projections, school recommendations, a 4-year course path, and a parent conversation guide. The report is generated by AI and emailed within 60 seconds, white-labeled with your name.

Can I try it before committing to a plan?

Yes. Every plan includes a 30-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start. If you decide it's not right for your practice, just cancel — no questions asked.

How does white-labeling work?

On the Growing Practice and School plans, you can upload your logo and set your practice name. Parent Reports sent through your account will display your branding — not FindYourMajor's. Students using your link see a co-branded experience. Your custom counselor link (findyourmajor.org/c/yourname) is included on all plans.

Does this work for independent college advisors or just school counselors?

Both. Independent advisors often find the most value because they can offer white-labeled reports as part of their client packages — adding a professional deliverable without adding hours of work. School counselors benefit most from the student dashboard and the ability to scale intake across hundreds of students.
